Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes, most of our beginner lessons and practice materials are free to access.

3. Do I need to create an account?

You can explore some content without an account, but creating one helps track your progress.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

Practice daily, use flashcards, complete exercises, and read regularly from our lesson materials.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Yes, certificates are provided after successfully completing selected structured courses.
